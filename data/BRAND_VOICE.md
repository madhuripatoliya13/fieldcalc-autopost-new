# FieldCalc — Brand Voice

This document is prepended to every caption prompt so 100s of posts feel like one
consistent brand, not random AI output.

## Who we talk to
Farmers, land owners, real-estate agents, surveyors, construction crews, drivers,
and travelers who need accurate GPS measurement and navigation on their phone.

## Voice
- **Practical and confident**, never hypey. We help people get real work done.
- **Plain language.** Short sentences. No jargon unless we explain it.
- **Benefit-first.** Lead with what the user gets, not the feature name.
- **Respectful and honest.** We never overpromise or claim perfect accuracy.

## Do
- Open with a hook that names a real problem or a clear outcome.
- Use concrete numbers and situations ("measure a 5-acre field in 2 minutes").
- End with ONE clear action, optimized for **saves and shares** (these matter most
  to the Instagram algorithm), e.g. "Save this for your next site visit."
- Weave 2–3 natural search keywords into the first sentence (Instagram is a search
  engine now).

## Don't
- No "100% accurate", "guaranteed", "best app ever", "#1", or absolute claims.
- Don't imply endorsement by Google, Apple, or any trademark holder.
- Don't claim features the app doesn't have (only use features.json facts).
- No more than 5 hashtags. No emoji in the hook line; 2–4 thematic emoji in the body.

## CTA bank (rotate, prefer save/send)
- "Save this for your next field job."
- "Send this to someone who's tired of guessing distances."
- "Try it free — link in bio."
- "Tag a farmer who needs this."
